# Access and Roles

Use this page to pick the correct role and entry path before you sign in.

## Who this is for

Anyone who is not sure which role they should use in the app.

## Before you start

1. Confirm your assignment with your organizer (chairperson, member, guest attendee, or admin).
2. Keep your login details ready (username/password or attendee access code).
3. If you are a guest attendee, make sure you have the meeting join link or QR.

## Step-by-step

1. Identify your task:
   - Run and moderate a meeting -> chairperson
   - Join and participate as committee member -> member
   - Join without committee account -> guest attendee
   - Manage accounts/committees/platform settings -> admin
2. Use the matching entry path:
   - Chairperson/member/admin: open the main login page and sign in with username/password.
   - Guest attendee (first join): open the meeting join page from the shared link/QR and complete signup.
   - Guest attendee (returning): open attendee login and enter your access code.
3. After sign-in, check that you landed in the right area for your role.
4. If you are in the wrong area, sign out and sign in again with the correct account.

## What you should see

- Chairperson: committee and moderation controls (meeting lifecycle, agenda, voting, attendee support).
- Member: meeting participation view and live meeting actions.
- Guest attendee: attendee login/join flow and then live meeting page.
- Admin: admin area for users, committees, and system settings.

## If something goes wrong

- You cannot find an expected action:
  Check your role first. Most missing controls are role restrictions.
- You reached the wrong area after login:
  Sign out and log back in with the intended account.
- You are an attendee and cannot continue:
  Ask the chairperson for a valid join link, a new access code, or a recovery QR.

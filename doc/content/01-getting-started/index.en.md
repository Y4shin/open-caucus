# Getting Started

Use this section to choose the correct onboarding guide for your role and current task.

![Home committees screenshot](../../assets/captures/app-home-committees.en.light.desktop.png)

## Who this is for

New users and returning users who need to pick the correct first-step documentation.

## Before you start

1. Know your role (chairperson, member, attendee, or admin).
2. Know your immediate goal (choose role path, sign in, or find more help).

## Step-by-step

1. Start with [Access and Roles](/docs/01-getting-started/01-access-and-roles) to confirm which guide applies to you.
2. Continue with [Login and Staying Signed In](/docs/01-getting-started/02-login-and-session-model) to sign in and understand what happens while you stay signed in.
3. Use [Help Search and Doc Navigation](/docs/01-getting-started/03-help-search-and-doc-navigation) to find task-specific guides while working.

## What you should see

- You can identify your role path before taking actions in the app.
- You can complete sign-in with the correct login method.
- You can navigate to deeper guides without guessing where to start.

## If something goes wrong

- You are unsure which guide to open first:
  Start with Access and Roles, then follow the sequence on this page.
- You still cannot find your task:
  Open Help in the app and search by task keywords (for example: join, vote, speaker, recovery).

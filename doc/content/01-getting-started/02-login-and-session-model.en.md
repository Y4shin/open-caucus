# Login and Staying Signed In

Use this page to sign in, understand what happens while you stay signed in, and sign out safely.

## Who this is for

Chairpersons, members, admins, and attendees who need to log in or recover from login issues.

## Before you start

1. Confirm your role and login method:
   - Chairperson/member/admin: username and password
   - Attendee: meeting access code
2. Use the correct login page for your role.
3. Keep your credentials ready before starting.

## Step-by-step

1. Open the correct login page:
   - Main login page for chairperson/member/admin accounts.
   - Attendee login page for meeting attendees with an access code.
2. Enter your credentials and submit the form.
3. Confirm you are redirected into the expected area for your role.
4. If you open a login page while already signed in, check that you are sent back to your working page.
5. When finished, use sign-out in the app.
6. Confirm sign-out succeeded by checking that protected pages now require login again.

## What you should see

- After successful login, you land in the area for your role.
- While you are signed in, opening login again sends you back to your working page.
- After sign-out, you return to a login screen.

## If something goes wrong

- Login form is rejected:
  Re-enter credentials carefully and check for typing/case issues.
- You keep landing in an unexpected area:
  Sign out first, then log in with the correct account.
- Attendee code does not work:
  Ask the chairperson for a fresh access code or recovery QR.
- You think you signed out but still have access:
  Refresh once; if access remains, sign out again and close/reopen the browser tab.

# Live Page Self-Service and Voting

![Live view with speakers](../../assets/captures/app-live-view-with-speakers.en.light.desktop.png)

Use this page during the meeting to speak, follow documents, and participate in votes.

## Who this is for

Members and guests who are already inside the live meeting page.

## Before you start

1. Join the meeting and open the live page.
2. Check that your name appears in the session header.
3. Wait for the chairperson to activate agenda points and open voting when needed.

## Layout on desktop vs mobile

- Desktop:
  Agenda, speakers, current document, and votes are easier to monitor at once.
- Mobile:
  The same controls are available in a vertical flow; focus on one section at a time.

## Manage your own speaking actions

### Step-by-step

1. Add yourself to the speakers queue:
   - Click **Add Myself** for a regular speech.
   - Use the **PO** button for a point-of-order entry when needed.
2. If you are currently speaking and finished:
   - Click **Yield Speech**.
3. Watch your row in the speakers list for your status and badges.

## Follow current meeting context

1. Check the active agenda point.
2. Open or download the current document when it is shown.
3. Stay on the live page during active discussion and voting.

## Participate in votes

### Open vote

1. Select your option(s) within the shown limits.
2. Click **Submit Open Ballot** before the vote closes.

### Secret vote

1. Select your option(s).
2. Click **Submit Secret Ballot**.
3. Keep your receipt/token if shown.

## If something goes wrong

- You cannot add yourself as speaker:
  Wait until an agenda point is active.
- Vote card shows not eligible:
  Ask the chairperson whether you joined before the vote opened.
- Submit button is disabled:
  Check whether the vote is still open and your selection is valid.
- Current document is missing:
  The chairperson may not have set one for this agenda point yet.
- Mobile feels hard to track:
  Complete speaking or voting actions first, then scroll for context details.

## What happens next

If you need to re-enter later, use [Guest Signup and Attendee Login](/docs/04-members-attendees/02-guest-signup-and-attendee-login). For chairperson-controlled recovery, ask for a recovery link or QR code.

# Live Page Self-Service and Voting

![Live view with speakers](../../assets/captures/app-live-view-with-speakers.en.light.desktop.png)

## What You Can Do

- Add yourself to the speakers list.
- Yield when you are done speaking.
- Follow the current document and agenda context.
- Participate in open and secret votes when available.

## Voting Behavior

- Select within the allowed limits shown on the vote.
- Submit before the vote closes.
- Keep your receipt/token if shown for later verification.

## Good Participant Practice

Stay on the live page during active voting and refresh only if prompted by the chairperson.

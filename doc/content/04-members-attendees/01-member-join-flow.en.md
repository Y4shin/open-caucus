# Member Join Flow

![Member join to live](../../assets/captures/app-member-join-to-live.en.light.desktop.gif)

Use this guide when you are a committee member joining an active meeting.

## Who this is for

Committee members with a regular member login (not guest access).

## Before you start

1. Sign in with your member account.
2. Open your committee dashboard.
3. Confirm that a meeting is currently active.

## Layout on desktop vs mobile

- Desktop:
  The active meeting card and join action are easy to access from the committee page.
- Mobile:
  The same actions are available, but cards are stacked vertically.

## Step-by-step

1. On the committee page, find the active meeting.
2. Click **Join Meeting**.
3. On the join page:
   - If this is your first join for this meeting, click **Sign Up**.
   - If you already joined before, click **Enter Meeting**.
4. Wait for redirect to the live meeting page.

## What you should see

- After successful join, you are taken to the live page.
- Your name appears in the session header on the live page.
- Returning members can re-enter without creating duplicate participant entries.

## If something goes wrong

- You cannot see **Join Meeting**:
  The meeting may not be active yet.
- You are sent to login again:
  Sign in first with your member account, then retry.
- Join action is missing or blocked:
  Ask the chairperson whether this meeting is available for joining.
- Mobile navigation feels unclear:
  Return to the committee page and re-open the active meeting card.

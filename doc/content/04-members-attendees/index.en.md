# Members and Attendees

Use this section to join meetings and participate safely once you are inside.

## Who this is for

Committee members and guest attendees.

## Before you start

1. Know whether you are joining as a member or as a guest.
2. Prepare the right credential:
   - member account login, or
   - guest access code / join link.

## Step-by-step

1. If you are a committee member, start with [Member Join Flow](/docs/04-members-attendees/01-member-join-flow).
2. If you are a guest, use [Guest Signup and Attendee Login](/docs/04-members-attendees/02-guest-signup-and-attendee-login).
3. After entering the meeting, use [Live Page Self Service and Voting](/docs/04-members-attendees/03-live-page-self-service-and-voting).

## What you should see

- A clear path for both member and guest access.
- Fewer failed joins and login retries.
- A consistent way to speak and vote on the live page.

## If something goes wrong

- You are unsure which page applies:
  Decide first whether you are joining as member or guest.
- You cannot re-enter:
  Use attendee login with your access code, or request recovery help from the chairperson.

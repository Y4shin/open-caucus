# Guest Signup and Attendee Login

![Guest signup form](../../assets/captures/app-guest-signup-form.en.light.desktop.png)

![Attendee login](../../assets/captures/app-attendee-login.en.light.desktop.png)

Use this guide if you are joining as a guest and do not have a member account.

## Who this is for

Guest participants who join through a chairperson-provided link or QR code.

## Before you start

1. Get the join link or join QR from the chairperson.
2. Keep your phone or browser ready to stay in the meeting page after join.

## Layout on desktop vs mobile

- Desktop:
  You can keep join/login pages open in one browser tab.
- Mobile:
  Join is usually easiest via QR scan, then continue in the same browser.

## First-Time Guest Signup

### Step-by-step

1. Open the join page from the shared link or QR code.
2. Enter your name in the guest form.
3. Optional: set **FLINTA*** if requested by the meeting rules.
4. Enter the meeting secret if it is not already prefilled.
5. Click **Sign Up as Guest**.
6. Save the shown access code.

### What you should see

- After signup, you are redirected to the live meeting page.
- Your guest identity is active for the current session.
- The access code can be reused later to log in again.

## Returning with an Access Code

### Step-by-step

1. Open the attendee login page.
2. Enter your access code.
3. Submit and continue to the live page.

## If you lose access

1. Ask the chairperson for a recovery link or recovery QR code.
2. Open that recovery link directly.
3. Confirm you return to the live page under your existing guest identity.

## If something goes wrong

- Signup fails with invalid meeting secret:
  Check the secret and retry, or request the join QR again.
- Login fails with invalid access code:
  Re-enter carefully, or ask for a recovery link.
- Guest signup is closed:
  Ask the chairperson to open signup temporarily.
- Mobile browser leaves the page:
  Reopen with your access code or recovery link.

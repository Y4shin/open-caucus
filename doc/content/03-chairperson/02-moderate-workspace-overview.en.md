# Moderation Page Overview

![Moderate overview](../../assets/captures/app-moderate-overview.en.light.desktop.png)

Use this page as your live control center while a meeting is running.

## Who this is for

Chairpersons who moderate meetings in real time.

## Before you start

1. Open the meeting from the committee dashboard and click **Manage**.
2. Ensure the intended meeting is active before moderation begins.
3. Keep this page open during the whole meeting.

## Layout on desktop vs mobile

- Desktop:
  Left side shows tabbed moderation controls (`Agenda`, `Tools`, `Attendees`, `Settings`) while the right side shows `Speakers` and `Add Speaker` at the same time.
- Mobile:
  Sections are stacked vertically. Use the left-side tabs first, then scroll to `Speakers` and `Add Speaker` below.

## Step-by-step

1. Get familiar with the left-side tabs:
   - **Agenda**: activate and edit agenda points.
   - **Tools**: manage files for the active point and run **Votes**.
   - **Attendees**: switch signup on/off, open join QR, and maintain the attendee list.
   - **Settings**: switch between meeting defaults and agenda-point overrides.
2. Use the right side continuously:
   - **Speakers** section: start/end speeches and monitor speaking order.
   - **Add Speaker** section: search attendees and add them to the queue quickly.
3. During the meeting, repeat this sequence:
   - Set/confirm the active agenda point.
   - Handle speakers and attendees.
   - Open/close votes when needed.
   - Return to agenda and repeat for the next point.
4. Use help buttons in sections when you need quick guidance for agenda or speakers tasks.
5. On mobile, follow a scroll rhythm:
   - Do left-tab actions first.
   - Scroll down to process speakers.
   - Scroll back up to switch tabs when needed.

## What you should see

- One page containing all core moderation controls without a full navigation change.
- Left tabs switch section content while keeping you in the same meeting page.
- On mobile, controls are the same but arranged in a vertical flow.
- Speakers and attendee/vote areas update as actions are submitted.

## If something goes wrong

- Speakers or votes actions are unavailable:
  Check whether an agenda point is active; many moderation actions depend on that.
- Participant join is blocked:
  Open the **Attendees** tab and confirm signup is enabled.
- You cannot find a control you expect:
  Verify you are on the correct left tab (`Agenda`, `Tools`, `Attendees`, `Settings`).
- Mobile feels cramped during moderation:
  Use one tab at a time, finish that action, then scroll to the next section instead of jumping between many controls.
- The page looks out of date during heavy activity:
  Use refresh controls in the affected section (for example in **Votes**), then continue.

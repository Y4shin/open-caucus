# Attendees Signup and Recovery

Use this page to manage who is in the meeting and quickly help guests who lost access.

## Who this is for

Chairpersons who manage attendee signup during a live meeting.

## Before you start

1. Open the meeting moderation page.
2. Go to the **Attendees** tab.
3. Decide whether you need to:
   - open/close guest signup
   - add/remove attendees
   - help a guest recover access

## Layout on desktop vs mobile

- Desktop:
  You can manage attendees while still seeing other moderation areas nearby.
- Mobile:
  Open the **Attendees** tab first, finish attendee updates there, then scroll to other sections.

## Manage Signup and Attendees

### Step-by-step

1. Control guest signup:
   - In **Attendees**, switch **Guest signup** on or off.
   - Keep it on while people are joining.
   - Switch it off when joining should stop.
2. Share the join page quickly:
   - Click **Show signup QR**.
   - Ask guests to scan the QR code to open the join page.
3. Add a guest manually (if needed):
   - In **Add guest**, enter the display name.
   - Optionally set **Gender quoted**.
   - Click **Add**.
4. Update attendee flags:
   - Use the attendee switches to set **Chair** where needed.
   - For guests, use **Gender quoted** if required by your rules.
5. Remove duplicates or mistakes:
   - Click **Remove attendee** on the wrong entry and confirm.

### What you should see

- Signup status changes immediately.
- Added guests appear in the attendee list right away.
- Chair and gender-quoted switches update each attendee row.
- QR opens a join page guests can use on their phone.

## Recovery for Guests Who Lost Access

Use this only for guest attendees.

### Step-by-step

1. In the attendee list, find the guest and open **Recovery link**.
2. On the recovery page, copy the recovery link or show the recovery QR code.
3. Share that link/QR directly with the same guest.
4. Ask the guest to open it and continue to attendee login.

### What to confirm

- The guest can open the link and reach attendee login.
- The guest can re-enter without creating a duplicate attendee entry.

## If something goes wrong

- Guests cannot join:
  Check that **Guest signup** is switched on.
- Recovery option is missing for a person:
  Recovery links are only available for guest attendees.
- A person appears twice:
  Keep one correct entry and remove the duplicate.
- Changes seem hard to track on mobile:
  Do attendee tasks first in the **Attendees** tab, then scroll to other sections.
